Project Overview

An advertising campaign designed to bring the Kafalah program's vision to life by transforming 2D architectural blueprints into vivid 3D achievements, proving that with the right financing, ambition becomes reality.

The Challenge

01

The challenge was to find a visually impactful way to illustrate how the Kafalah program helps turn abstract business plans and ideas into real, tangible success stories for entrepreneurs.

The Creative Solution

02

The "Blueprint Concept" used a powerful visual metaphor where each ad shows a business idea (a restaurant, factory, etc.) literally rising from its own paper blueprint. The campaign ran under the inspiring hashtag #حدك_السما (The sky's your limit).
