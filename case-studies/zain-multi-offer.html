Project Overview

A unified campaign designed to tackle multiple telecom products—from roaming packs and youth bundles to portable Wi-Fi—all crafted for action across different segments.

The Challenge

01

The challenge was to promote a diverse range of telecom products under a single, cohesive campaign umbrella. The campaign needed to maintain a consistent brand voice while addressing the unique needs of different customer segments for various services.

The Creative Solution

02

The creative solution was to ensure that while each ad addressed a different need, the tone remained consistent: youthful, bold, and benefit-first.

By using a unified visual style and direct messaging, the campaign successfully communicated multiple offers without diluting the brand's strong, digital-first identity.
