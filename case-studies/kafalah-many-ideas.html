Project Overview

This project represents the annual creative process of reimagining Kafalah's mission. It involved developing multiple new visual concepts and narratives to tell a fresh story of support, trust, and entrepreneurial ambition.

The Challenge

01

The challenge was to consistently bring Kafalah's mission to life in a new way each year. This required creating distinct visual concepts that felt fresh and engaging to the entrepreneurial audience while always reinforcing the core message of support.

The Creative Solution

02

The solution was to develop multiple, distinct concepts under one theme. This included a "modular building blocks" campaign to represent building on success, and another based on "handcrafted models" to show passion and detail. Each concept told a new story while being rooted in the single, powerful message: "Kafalah backs your growth."
